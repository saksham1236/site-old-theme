The Young Funds Program is a initiative that aims to educate students about investing from an early age. The website redesign was part of a school project that involved us contacting various companies and asking if we could redesign site as a part of our term project and in the end they would choose whether they want to implement the changes or not.

The Problem

The first part of the project involved looking at the website and identifying potential issues that hinder the experience of people on the website. So we as a group of four people made individual assessments about the website design and created a report based on our findings.

We also noticed that there were many design inconsistencies between different pages and random animations hindered the flow and the user experience of the website. There were also various issues with content organization and repetition around the website.

The Planning

For the planning phase we laid out the objectives for the redesign and the four of us created low fidelity mock-ups on an individual level and then came back to see if could make something greater out of the four designs.
